Data Upload

Do you want to upload data to ImmPort?

If you are interested in using ImmPort as a means to share your data with the scientific community, please contact our NIAID Program officers Quan Chen (quan.chen@nih.gov), Gabriel Rosenfeld (rosenfeldge@niaid.nih.gov), or Dawei Lin (dawei.lin@nih.gov).

Data is stored at a secure hosting facility within NIAID at immport.niaid.nih.gov and remains private until you inform our team that it is ready to be shared. At that time, the data is distributed to the wider community via this website (www.immport.org) and Amazon AWS.

For more information about our data upload process, please see our documentation and instructional videos below. Need help? Please feel free to contact us by email at BISC_Helpdesk@niaid.nih.gov and we are happy to provide a walkthrough.

To start uploading data, you will need to register for an ImmPort account to create your private workspace .

Data Package Validator
The Validator is an online tool that checks the format and content of the files in the data package and reports issues.
If there are any errors in the validation report, fix the first errors that are indicated and rerun the validation process.
The errors encountered first may have effects on later files that are validated.
It is highly recommended to use the Validator before sending data into ImmPort as part of the data upload process.
